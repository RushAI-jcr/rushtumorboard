  - **Ascites**: Some ovarian cancer trials exclude moderate-to-large ascites. Check if ascites status is provided.

  - **Measurable disease**: Most trials require measurable disease per RECIST 1.1. If imaging status is provided, verify. If not, flag as needing confirmation.
  - **Autoimmune disease**: Immunotherapy trials exclude active autoimmune disease requiring systemic treatment in past 2 years.
  - **Prior malignancy**: Most trials exclude other active malignancies within 2-5 years.

  - **Specific comorbidities**: HIV, hepatitis B/C, organ transplant, active infection, uncontrolled diabetes -- check against trial criteria.
